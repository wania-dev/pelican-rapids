Risq x PAF Chapter

President: Ismail Sheikh

Rizq Organization is set to provide humanitarian aid during any crisis and to provide food and necessities for people in need. It has joined hands with The City School PAF Chapter to achieve that. The society spreads awareness about the ongoing injustices in the world and about the unfortunate children, women, and men suffering due to poverty. The main aim of TCS PAF students is to raise funds for donation which would be used in providing for the economically vulnerable masses.

List of Activities

Since the start of the year, TCS PAF has held various fundraising events. These events included a Rizq Fest which lasted for a week.
Rizq held a psychology session to spread awareness about mental health.
It hosted a concert and tickets were sold to the students of PAF Chapter It also consisted of a bake sale where students could set up food and game stalls.
It also hosted a cricket tournament, with a large audience and players from the cricket team.
It also hosted a public speaking event for students to speak up about the Palestine issue and raise awareness about the genocide.

Notable mentions:
TCS PAF was able to raise 43,500 PKR in September, resulting in them being the second-best school throughout Pakistan for the highest number of donations. In October, TCS PAF climbed the ladder and became the first/top school with 50,000 PKR in donations. These donations have helped various people and countries around the globe. Rizq aims to continue this actively.
